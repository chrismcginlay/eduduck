    2. Paid - Students pay to enroll on your course. You set the fee. For your remuneration you will receive a reservation of the first Â£x of subscriptions followed by a percentage of all further subscriptions. See examples/try our calculator
    3. Closed - Your course is available only to your employees or staff. Your employees pay nothing. You pay a fee based on the number of students enrolling.

    * NB Free/Open courses must have their materials licenced under CC-by-SA or GNU FDL and you must have authority to issue such a licence
