EduDuck's Goal

To bring well organised, accessible, and useful learning experiences on a range of subjects leading up to college or university level study. All ages, income levels and geographical areas will be catered for.

EduDuck's Intended Audience

Eduduck aims to provide online courses roughly between the high school and college or university skill level. Many of our intended audience will be between 15 and 30 years old.

EduDuck's Course Categories

The courses will likely be of a technical nature. At this stage, there will be only one or perhaps two courses.
